Try PollEndpoint, an high performance, open-source question platform by Licson Lee.

Hi Performance

PollEndpoint uses node.js, a platform for data-intensive applications, to handle the load. It's highly-scalable and event-driven that makes it fast and reliable.

Search engines optimized

PollEndpoint is optimized for search engines to allow your questions to be spread widely.

We adopt the use of HTML5 microdata to let search engines have a better understanding of the questions.

Easy to setup

No accounts required. Just create the questions and that's it. (Statistics are public to everyone)

User-friendly interface

Answer your questions in a natural way! No ads on every page and we'll never have ads.
